The Imaginary Type Project Information

When you picture type, what do you see?

Do you see the font option window in your word processor? Do you see metal type in trays or a letterpress? Do you see the personality of each typeface you encounter: the playfulness of Comic Sans, the straight-fowardness of Helvetica? Or do you see files, the data sets that make up a typeface?

There are many right answers and opinions. Today, however, the most factual picture of type is this data. And that is what this project shows, the image of type today, featuring some of the most recognizable typefaces in existence.

Its coordinates, its file size, the number of counterspaces, the number of straight lines, and more, as typefaces have grown from metal type to files stored on our computers.
